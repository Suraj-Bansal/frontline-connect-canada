About Frontline Connect

‘No visitor’ policies have been implemented for COVID-19 infection control, thereby isolating patients from their care providers and families. We are equipping healthcare providers and patients with phones and tablets to:

Help patients and healthcare workers alike stay connected
Assist healthcare providers in delivering better care
Increasing family involvement and virtual connection with patients
